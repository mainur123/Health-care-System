ABOUT

Health Care System is the best tool for both doctors and patients. This is a powerful medical doctor appointment scheduling software to meet all challenges that may surface during your appointment scheduling. The process of booking appointments and scheduling management of Health Care System is easy for both the doctors and patients. This website also serve the user by giving Health Tips and Primary Aids.

Hello Doctors!!

You have to register in our website with your necessary details like name,designation,speciality,department name.After successfully registration you can log in with your username,password and can see your patient list who request for your appointment.

Hello Patients!!

You can see the Doctors list by regitration in our website.Then you have to submit a request for appointment of your desired doctor.
